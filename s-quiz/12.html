Sonu's age is the cube of a whole number. It was square of the same whole number four years ago. How long he must wait before his age is again the cube of a whole number ?
A.
12 years
B.
19 years
C.
17 years
D.
23 years

Your Answer: Option (Not Answered)

Correct Answer: Option B

Explanation

8 = 2 × 2 × 2 (a cube number)
Four years ago
8 - 4 = 4 = 2 × 2 (Square of the same whole number)
Next perfect cube number
27 = 3 × 3 × 3
⇒ 27 - 8 = 19 years
So, he should wait for another 19 years
