7.

If CGL = 295
CSE = 135
SSC = 205
Then, CLERK = ?
A.
430
B.
860
C.
220
D.
1020

Your Answer: Option (Not Answered)

Correct Answer: Option A

Explanation

CGL ⇒ C + G + L = 24 + 20 +15 = 59 ⇒ 59 × 5=295
CSE ⇒ C + S + E = 3 + 19 + 5 = 27 ⇒ 27 × 5=135
SSC ⇒ S + S + C = 19 + 19 + 3 = 41 ⇒ 41 × 5 = 205
CLERK ⇒ C+L+E+R+K ⇒ 24 + 15 + 22 + 9+ 16 = 86 ⇒ 86 × 5= 430

8. If 2A = 3B = 5C, then find the value of A : B : C .?
A.
6:10:15
B.
30:12:7
C.
6:5:2
D.
15:10:6

Your Answer: Option (Not Answered)

Correct Answer: Option D

Explanation

2A = 3B = 5C, LCM = 30
⇒ A = 30 2 =15, B = 30 3 =10 and C = 30/5 = 6
So, A : B : C = 15 : 10 : 6
